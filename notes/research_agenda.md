# Research Agenda — Next Run (Run 2)

## Priority Queue (inside-out order)

1. **Upgrade Road confidence (🟡→🟢).** Highest-value task: road is 68% of the
   Big Number and currently a residual. Triangulate with IEA Mobility model +
   national travel surveys (US NHTS, UK NTS, EU). Split into cars / 2-3-wheelers
   / buses with independent pkm.
2. **Rail HSR capsule.** Isolate high-speed rail (15% of pkm, outsized AHV per
   traveller). China/Japan/EU operator data.
3. **Air haul split capsule.** Short-haul vs long-haul pkm + block speeds.
4. **Historic reconstruction (the "over time" arc).** Build v̄ back through the
   jet age (1950+), auto age (1900+), rail age (1830+), pre-industrial baseline.
   This is the project's headline narrative — humanity's acceleration curve.
5. **Snapshot lens for Road + Air.** Occupancy × time-use → instantaneous
   people-in-motion; reconcile against the flow-lens AHV (the sanity gate).

## Revisions Needed
- Water pkm (ferry avg trip distance, cruise voyage model) — low stakes, 🔴→🟡.
- Active travel — replace per-capita assumptions with survey-based trip data;
  scope the ambient-movement extension as its own methodology.

- Naval subcategory (carrier / submarine / surface-combatant crews).

- OWID transport datasets (air/rail pkm timeseries — good for historic).
- National time-use surveys (ATUS, UK NTS) for snapshot occupancy.
- UITP for urban transit (metro/bus) pkm.
